Art:

Are you a creative person or would you like to be even more? Here are some things you can do to develop that ability.

Discover new music:
It's time to expand your musical tastes and discover other genres and artists that you didn't even imagine you might like. Maybe you'll be surprised!
  • Play your music in shuffle.
  • Ask your friends.
  • Post a question story on social media.

    Learn to play an instrument:
    If you always wanted to learn to play an instrument, now is your time
    Ask if someone in your family has an instrument you can take to practice
    Maybe a friend or a relative can teach you.
    Create something with clay:
    If you are not quite into painting, you can try to do some pottery with clay.
    Create your own decorations for your house!
    Create your own songs:
    If you already play an instrument or you live with someone who does, you can create your own songs.
    each person composes in their own way, find the one that suits you best and get down to work
    Who knows, maybe you create a hit.
    Decorate your house:
    Tired of the same old thing?
    Take advantage and give a fresh look to your house.
    Buy something online or reuse something old,
    maybe you can give it a new use.
    Customize your stuff
    Give your personal touch to your things:
    customize your clothes and shoes,
    paint something on the window, in the closet or on the wall...
