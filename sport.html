Sport:

For you who like to exercise and you may even practice a sport we recommend some apps to stay fit and some tips:

30 Day Fitness Challenge

With this app you can start 30-day exercise routines, being able to focus these exercises on the area you want to work on: abs, buttock, arms, legs or full body. Also in each of these you have 3 levels according to your level: beginner, intermediate or advanced.
And the best of all is that you will not need any extra material, you will work with things that you can find in your house.

Download for IOS Android

7 Minutes Training

It is based on High Intensity Circuit Training or HICT, and is very appropriate for those looking for intense workouts that last a short time. The application proposes 12 routines with periods of 30 seconds, after which you have rest periods of 10 seconds.
It has blocks of exercises divided into categories, although some will have to be unlocked by watching advertising videos or doing exercises from the Original category. The application will always explain in detail how you have to do each exercise.

Download for IOS Android

Pocket Yoga

If you want to do a little more in the world of Yoga, this other application allows you to select from isolated exercises to complete classes. In all of them, you will have videos that explain all the movements, each of which is also perfectly cataloged and detailed step by step.
This allows if you are still a beginner you can start trying them, and if you are already an expert you also have information to correct and improve. In short, a very complete Yoga application to enter your world.

Download for IOS Android

HIIT y Cardio

This app is based on High Intensity Interval Training or HIIT to allow you to do cardio workouts at home and without any equipment. It has 4 training programs and a total of 90 exercises, with video tutorials, voice trainer, customizable workouts and various types of difficulties to adapt to you.
Focused on increasing muscle strength, balance and heart status.

Download for IOS Android
